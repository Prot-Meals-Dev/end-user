Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures. However, no method of online transmission is 100% secure.

4. Sharing Your Information

We share your details only with trusted delivery partners and payment providers, solely for fulfilling your orders.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or wish to exercise your privacy rights, please visit our Contact Us page.
